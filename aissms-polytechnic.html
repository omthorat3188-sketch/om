Our Mission

To produce diploma holders of repute by providing world-class physical and academic infrastructure, instilling research temperament, and nurturing holistic development through student-centred education.

1994Founded

AISSMS Polytechnic was founded in July 1994 by the All India Shri Shivaji Memorial Society — an institution with deep roots in Maharashtra's educational heritage going back to 1917. Located on Kennedy Road, Pune, we offer diploma programmes across eight engineering disciplines.

Affiliated with MSBTE Mumbai and approved by AICTE New Delhi, the institute blends rigorous academics with cutting-edge practical training to prepare students for thriving careers in industry.

Admission Process
2025–26

  • 1

    Complete 10th Standard

    Eligibility requires completion of SSC (10th) with Mathematics & Science. Direct 2nd Year via HSC (12th).

  • 2

    Fill DTE Online Form

    Register and submit the online application via the Maharashtra DTE portal. No entrance exam required for first year.

  • 3

    Centralised Counselling

    Seats are allotted through the state centralised merit-based counselling process based on 10th marks.

  • 4

    Document Verification

    Visit campus with originals — 10th Marksheet, school leaving certificate, identity proof, and category documents.

  • 5

    Fee Payment & Enrolment

    Pay admission fees to confirm your seat. Last date typically falls in September each year.
